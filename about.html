Modular Office Furniture Specification

An interagency subcommittee of the Sustainable Building Task Force finalized a groundbreaking environmental specification for modular office furniture purchased under State contract. The specification sets new industry standards for indoor air quality, prescribes a high level of recycled content in components and packaging, Environmental Requirements (Section 01350), an environmental specification that establishes sustainable building criteria and guides material selection.
